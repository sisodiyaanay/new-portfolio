ExperienceWhere I've been designing

Enterprise AI, end to end

One role, two very different products: a governance platform for teams running AI/ML infrastructure, and a knowledge platform built from the AI work engineers do every day.

SmartSense Consulting Solutions
Product Designer
Jun 2025 – Present · GIFT City, Gandhinagar, India
Ostrich AIenterprise AI governance & MLOps platform

An enterprise AI platform enabling organizations to govern AI systems, manage datasets, train and deploy ML models, and access decentralized GPU infrastructure through secure role-based workflows.

  • Cut the model deployment flow from 6 steps to 3 across two rounds of internal usability testing with ML engineers, then applied the same step-reduction approach to dataset creation, inferencing and infrastructure management workflows.
  • Owned interaction design across five product surfaces (VaultNest Studio, VaultNest Enterprise, Data Borough, Admin Panel and FlockChain), turning client Product Owner and PM requirements into production-ready interaction models.
  • Designed role-based experiences for 8+ enterprise personas, shipping 740+ high-fidelity screens across 78+ user flows tailored to each persona's permissions and responsibilities.
  • Built a shared design system, including a reusable component library, variables, and adaptive light and dark themes, deployed across 20+ dashboard pages and adopted as the consistency standard across all five sub-products.
  • Partnered with 2 client Product Owners, 2 Product Managers and 22 software engineers to turn evolving requirements into interaction specifications, and mentored a junior Product Designer through 6 months of the engagement.
Second BrainAI-native enterprise knowledge platform

An AI-native enterprise knowledge platform enabling engineering teams to discover organizational knowledge, understand AI-assisted development workflows and accelerate software delivery through conversational interfaces and developer intelligence.

  • Contributed to Second Brain as one of seven members of SmartSense's AI Innovation Pod, working directly with the CEO and cross-functional leadership to take an early concept to internal release in two months, with capture live across 100+ engineers and 15,000+ synced Claude Code sessions.
  • Owned end-to-end interaction design across conversational retrieval, enterprise search, session history and document intelligence, defining the core turn-based unit that made opaque, autonomous AI agent activity legible to any teammate, across 25+ user flows spanning three roles.
  • Conceived and led design for the Session Viewer, a flagship surface with five ways to view a session, prototyping the MVP to validate the interaction model before engineering build.
  • Designed the Leadership layer with a structural privacy model: aggregates only, no drill-down into individual sessions, built into what each role can access rather than as a toggle.
  • Rebuilt the daily mirror after v1 surfaced unverifiable claims, then established an evidence-linking pattern, so every insight is traceable to its source, now used across all surfaces.
