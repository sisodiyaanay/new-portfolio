Some hacks, if you dont have the data  
**1\. Reverse-Engineer the Information Architecture (IA)**

If you don't have early wireframes, you must show the underlying skeleton of your final product. Case study readers needs to see that you understand how systems are structured before they are painted.

* **Create System Maps:** Take your final product and draw a flowchart of how the data moves. For the **Session Viewer**, map out how a raw JSON log translates into the "Story Canvas."  
* **Deconstruct the UI (The "Wireframe" Hack):** Take your polished, dark-mode screens and strip them of all color, typography, and styling in Figma. Turn them into gray boxes. Use these gray-box screens to annotate the spatial layout and explain *why* elements are grouped the way they are. This proves structural thinking.
